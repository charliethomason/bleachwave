Summary: The Plum Tree Group are a Web Development, Marketing, and Software Development agency, based in the Chicago Loop. They specialize in Magento e-Commerce websites and new media solutions for small businesses. I originally joined PT in Fall 2011 as an Internet Marketing Specialist, to help with client strategy as well as social media and email marketing. After a month, I began to be involved more with internal Marketing initiatives, which were centered around a new website and branding campaign that had been recently been launched. I helped develop the Blog (later renamed “PT Voice”), which was built upon Plum Tree’s homemade content management system, eIntelli Catalyst. Much of the Blog content (through January 2012) was also written and published by me, which required use of HTML, CSS, and Adobe Photoshop. In addition, I conceptualized, directed, and edited a series of YouTube videos designed to help develop a community around the PT brand, as well as show authority in current events of the tech industry.
