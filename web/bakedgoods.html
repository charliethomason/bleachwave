Summary: As a full-time independent contractor for Imagination Publishing, a custom publishing agency located in the West Loop of Chicago, I helped develop custom Facebook content for a Fortune 50 Consumer Packaged Goods company. This required the use of HTML, CSS3, FBML (Facebook Markup Language â€” now defunct), Adobe Flash, ActionScript 2.0, and Adobe Photoshop. The original designs for all custom Facebook content would be mocked-up by either myself or the graphic design department and then I would make those designs come to life using a variety of tools. There were also instances where, due to a major Facebook design overhaul, it was necessary for modifications of existing custom content. I helped launch Pages for many different brands and initiatives, and even became involved with content strategy for many of these initiatives. Later, I also became responsible for measuring Google Analytics and other metrics data, as well as updating outbound link URLS in all Facebook content to allow for expanded social media metrics tracking. Overall, these initiatives were hugely successful and some Pages boasted hundreds of thousands of Likes.
